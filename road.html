Road Bikes

What you need to know.

Road bike cover everything from competitive racing bikes to your everyday commuter. On this page, you will find everything you need to know from the bike and safety gear to tips and tricks to scenic routes around the beautiful mountain roads of Sligo. As with all the other disciplines, safety is of huge importance when riding road and commuter bikes so be sure to pick yourself out all of the gear you may need to have a great adventure.

Enjoy your ride!
